Detailed Description

Slonczewski Spin Transfer Torque model.

\[ \boldsymbol{H}_{stt} = - \frac{j_e \hbar}{2 e \mu_0 M_s t_{fl}} [\eta_{damp} \boldsymbol{m} \times \boldsymbol{p} + \eta_{field} \boldsymbol{p}] \]

where \(j_e\) is the current in [A], \(\hbar\) Planck constant, \(e\) is the electron charge, \(\eta_{damp}\) and \(\eta_{field}\) are damping and field like constants in [a.u.], \(\boldsymbol{p}\) is the spin polarization as unit length vector field in [a.u.], \(t_{fl}\) is the free layer thickness in [m].
